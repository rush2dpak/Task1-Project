Our recruitment process

1.  Intake: We get to know you, what you want, your team and culture fit and job requirements.
2. Communication advice: choice of recruitment channels (our extensive databases, websites, social media, print media, job boards etc.), choice of recruitment activities (advertising, posting, searching, networking, etc.), and creation of customized ads.
3. Interview: Assess the skills, attitudes and competencies of potential candidates and ensure they're a right match for your role. Reference check.
4. Talent Match: Provide you with a shortlist of pre-qualified & trade test passed candidates, Candidate selection & negotiation.
